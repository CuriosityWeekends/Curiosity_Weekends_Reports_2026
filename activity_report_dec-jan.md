**Prepared By:** Muhammed Afkar M A\

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 1. Introduction

During the last two months, Curiosity Weekends has continued to function
as an active learning and innovation space for students and young
enthusiasts. The organization has focused on strengthening technical
skills, promoting collaborative learning, and encouraging practical
project-based education.

The main objective during this period was to improve participation,
organize learning through study circles, document member activities, and
create a proper system for monitoring progress.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 2. Overview of Activities

Curiosity Weekends conducted regular learning and project sessions.
Members actively participated in technical discussions, hands-on
experiments, and collaborative project development.

The space remained open for self-learning, group study, mentoring
activities, and peer support. Special emphasis was given to maintaining
a structured and disciplined learning environment.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 3. Study Circles and Learning Groups

To promote focused and systematic learning, Curiosity Weekends
established organized study circles. These groups supported subject-wise
learning and peer mentoring.

### Active Study Circles

-   Mathematics Study Circle\
-   Physics Study Circle\
-   General Science and Technology Group

These study circles conducted regular problem-solving sessions, concept
discussions, and exam-oriented preparations. The system improved
consistency, leadership, and academic performance among members.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 4. Projects and Technical Development

Members worked on multiple academic and technical projects related to
electronics, automation, programming, and applied sciences.

Major project activities included:

-   Idea planning and documentation\
-   Component selection and testing\
-   Coding and debugging\
-   Prototype development\
-   Performance evaluation

Both individual and team-based projects were encouraged. Project
tracking systems were introduced to monitor progress, tools used, and
outcomes.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 5. Item Management and Resource Tracking

An item and resource management system was implemented to ensure
responsible use of equipment.

The system records:

-   Issued items\
-   Quantity and purpose\
-   Return status\
-   Condition of equipment

This improved accountability and reduced loss of resources.
